they are different, if they are. Explain why they can have different parameters 
and be equivalent, if they are equivalent.

    //code fragment 1
    $("li").each(function(idx, e) {
    $(e).css(“color”, “yellow”); });
    //code fragment 2
    $("li").each(function() {
    $(this).css(“color”, “yellow”); });
    //code fragment 3
    $("li").each(function(idx) {
    $(this).css(“color”, “yellow”); });
